Who am I?

2-D, Stuart Pot or Pot, is the lead vocalist and keyboard / piano manager for the British virtual band Gorillaz. Like all the members of the band, it was created by Damon Albarn and Jamie Hewlett. In 1998, Murdoc and 2-D moved to Kong Studios and convinced Russel Hobbs to join them as their drummer. At first, Paula Cracker, 2-D's girlfriend, was the group's guitarist, but was kicked out of the group after Russel found her having sex with Murdoc in the studio bathrooms. This sparked 2-D's anger and led to the breakdown of their relationship. Later, 2-D began dating Rachel Stevens, a member of S-Club 7, and invited her to the group's first concert at the Camden Brownhouse. After Gorillaz attended the signing meeting with the music label, Rachel ended up with 2-D the next day after Murdoc hit her overnight to get her attention.
